Local Hotels

Virginia Park Lodge Hotel

Located 15 km from Loughcrew Historical Gardens & Visitor Centre, Virginia Park Lodge provides accommodation with free WiFi and free private parking. Some units are air conditioned and include a seating area with a flat-screen TV and/or iPod docking station. A continental, vegetarian or vegan breakfast is available daily at the property. The country house offers a terrace.

Lakeside Manor

Featuring free WiFi, Lakeside Manor Hotel is located on the Cavan/Meath border, 11 km from Loughcrew Historical Gardens & Visitor Centre. Free private parking is available on site. Every room at this hotel is air conditioned and is fitted with a TV. Relagh Beg is 8 km from Lakeside Manor Hotel, while Moylagh Church and Castle is 10 km from the property. Dublin Airport and city are both under an hour's drive away.

St. Kyrans Country House

Situated in Virginia, 16 km from Loughcrew Historical Gardens & Visitor Centre, St Kyrans Guest House features accommodation with a garden, free private parking, a shared lounge and a terrace.
